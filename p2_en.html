Practice as chemotherapy

1 Sleep in a comfortable position. Avoid moving or lifting arm received chemotherapy.

2 When there is a pain, swelling, heat around in area inject to chemotherapy. Notify your Physician or Nurse immediately.

3 In the case of leakage at area inject to chemotherapy. Lift the leakage of fluid to lower level than the heart.

4 Observe the droplet rate of chemotherapy. If the droplet rate of chemotherapy is uneven please notify physician or nurse.
